Where Do Trends Come From?

A trend is defined as a path or direction in which something is heading. As such, global trends are predictions for the future of the world, be it environmentally or socially. Trends come from a mixture of the scientific analysis of data and expert intuition. Typically, trends are displayed in the form of models, based on past information and current innovations on our world.

Global Trends

There are an infinite number of possibilities for what our future may look like. However, some predictions for our world are much more likely than others, even in a universe full of surprises. Global trends are dictated by innovations in technology, evolution in our social dynamics, and our responses to both of these things. Currently, our ability to develop new technologies and evolve as a culture are catalytic. Every day we change as a species and as a world.
