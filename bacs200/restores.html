ReStores

Habitat for Humanity has retail stores across the country! These locations take donations and sell goods at discounted prices to secure revenue for HFHI projects. Anyone is welcome to visit or donate. While these items can often be used or have less than perfect conditions, there are often brand new items being sold at discount prices!
