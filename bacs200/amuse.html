The longest sentence ever signed by a monkey

Nim Chimpsky was a monkey who was taught sign language in order to communicate with people.

The longest recorded sentence from Nim was as follows:

"Give orange me give eat orange me eat orange give me eat orange give me you."

There is an edited documentary video that (exaggeratingly) shows this sentence

Watch it now!
