Habitat for Humanity International

Creating housing, stability, and opportunity

Result of efforts

Since its founding in 1976, Habitat for Humanity has helped over 29 million people improve their housing conditions (2019). This includes building over 800,000 homes. These efforts could not have come about without the help of over 1.4 million volunteers (2013). Every year, millions of people benefit from the work of this organization. Join the cause today!

How to apply to be a Habitat Homeowner

Everyone can apply to use the resources HFHI offers for home creation/ renovation. In order to qualify, one needs to demonstrate that they are in need of safe, affordable housing (needs vary by community). For more information, click here or call 1-800-HABITAT (1-800-422-4828).
