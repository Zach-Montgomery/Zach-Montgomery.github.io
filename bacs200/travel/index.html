Habitat for Humanity International

Creating housing, stability, and opportunity

Their Vision

Habitat for Humanity aims to provide affordable housing in order to benefit individuals and communities alike. Through bringing together volunteers and homeowners, this organization works tirelessly to spread peace, hope, compassion, and dignity to the world. HFHI works to ensure everyone has a place to call home. In an effort to fulfill this vision, Habitat for Humanity creates opportunities for volunteers to gain experience and meet people, provides assistence in constructing reliable and stable housing, and raises money through their non-profit retail stores.

Being Faithful Stewards

Nothing can sum up the motivation and actions taken by Habitat for Humanity better than Christ-like. One of the goals of this organization is to spread the love of God across the world through creating shelters. However, the actions taken are meant to promote love, not conversion to any faith. While HFHI utilizes Christian principlies in their operations, they do not proselytize.
