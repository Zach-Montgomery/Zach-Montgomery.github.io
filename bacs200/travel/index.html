Package Choices:

Standard

The Standard Package is the most basic and cost-effective travel option. However, it still offers two fun activities to enjoy, a classy hotel room, and a means of transportation across several beautiful overlooks.

Deluxe

The Deluxe Package is comprised of a more luxurious experience. It offers three fun activities, a cozy bed and breakfast in a gorgeous meadow right outside of town, transportation, a guided tour of scenic overlooks and a few national parks.

Premium

The Premium Package includes everything in the Deluxe and Standard Packages, plus more! It holds five activities, a stunning hotel with a beatiful view, tours for up to eight different locations, ranging from fascinating tours of some of New Zealand's finest attractions to immaculate scenic forests and landscapes, and a $500 room service budget!
