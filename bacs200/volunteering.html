Volunteering

Are you interested in changing the world? Here are some opportunities for you! Habitat for Humanity International has many different programs for all walks of life. Here are some of the many options for volunteering for HFHI!

  • Volunteer locally: Discover programs happening near you! This ranges from helping with building projects to assisting in running one of Habitat for Humanity's retail stores, where they new and used goods to help fund their ventures. Learn more about opportunities near you

  • Help around the world: Look for ways to travel the world and build with Habitat for Humanity! From building at different sites across the U.S. to immersing yourself in creating or repairing homes in different cultures, discover what your next adventure holds! Find out about global opportunities

  • Building events: Beyond the scheduled building and renovations projects, HFHI hosts several continual or annual events! These include community renovation programs, youth involvement activities, Home Builders Blitz, and more! Register for building events
